Privacy Policy

GoBetterment App Privacy Protection Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. Purpose of Personal Information Collection and Use

GoBetterment collects and uses the following personal information to provide services for personal growth and self-development.

Personal Information Collected

  • Basic Information: Email address, username
  • App Usage Data: Goal setting, progress tracking, achievement records
  • Device Information: Device identifier, operating system version

Purpose of Use

  • Service provision and improvement
  • Providing personalized experiences
  • Customer support and inquiry response
  • Security and fraud prevention

2. Personal Information Retention and Use Period

Collected personal information is immediately destroyed after the purpose of service provision is achieved. However, if preservation is required by relevant laws and regulations, it will be retained for the specified period.

3. Third-Party Provision of Personal Information

GoBetterment does not provide users' personal information to third parties in principle. However, exceptions are made in the following cases:

  • When the user has given prior consent
  • When required by law or when investigation agencies request it for investigation purposes through procedures and methods prescribed by law

4. Security Measures for Personal Information

GoBetterment takes the following measures to protect personal information:

  • Encryption of personal information
  • Technical measures against hacking
  • Restriction of access to personal information
  • Minimization and training of personnel handling personal information

5. Access, Correction, Deletion, and Suspension of Personal Information

Users can request access, correction, deletion, and suspension of processing of their personal information. Please contact us at the address below for any requests.

6. Personal Information Protection Officer

To oversee personal information processing and handle complaints and damage relief related to personal information processing, we have designated a personal information protection officer as follows:

  • Personal Information Protection Officer
  • Email: gobetterment@gmail.com
  • Developer: gobetterment@gmail.com

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies from the effective date. If there are additions, deletions, or corrections to the content due to laws and policies, we will notify you through announcements 7 days before the implementation of changes.
